About me

Hello , I am Yu Pei Lin

I am a senior process engineer specializing in lithography with over 3.5 years of valuable experience in the semiconductor manufacturing industry. I have been a part of TSMC since obtaining my master's degree. Notably, I have been involved in both N12/N16 fab high volume manufacturing (HVM) and the advancement of N3 technology fab ramping to HVM. My role has allowed me to gain extensive expertise in optimizing lithography processes, enhancing yields, and leveraging productivity-enhancing Continuous Improvement Programs (CIP).



Recently, I embarked on a new journey to learn web development programming as a crucial component of my career development plan. I have acquired proficiency in a range of technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express, SQL, and NoSQL. My aim is to transition into a software engineering role as soon as possible
